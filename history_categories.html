History Categories

For the better understanding of history, five different categories were made to classify the information.

  • 1. Space:

    The space or territory where the events happened. The characteristics of the area might have affected that happened.

  • 2. Structure:

    It is basically what happened. All the actions that conform this event. For example, comparing it to a movie, the structure would be the plot.

  • 3. Duration:

    It is the amount of time the event lasted, from where it started to where it ended.

  • 4. Conjuncture:

    It is an important success that happened during the event the helps the understanding of it. Without this, the story wouldn’t make sense. It can also be explained as the context to comprehend what happens.

  • 5. Time:

    It is the moment where the event takes place. For example, the year 1854 on August 15th, at 6:00 p.m. This information can help to understand the success.
