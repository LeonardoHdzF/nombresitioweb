The definition of history is basically every event or action that happened in the past, the word comes from the word “Histor” in Greek which means “Witness/who saws”, but it has many definitions.

It has many divisions which include world history, national history, personal history, history of communities, and many others.

‘Herodotus’ is considered the father of sciences and therefore, of history.

When experts apply the scientific method in the study of history, the historiography is made.

In historiography, the history is studied as a science, using observation, theories, laws, and by different points of view.
